Our Journey

From our humble beginnings in Chandigarh to serving 100+ labs globally, discover the milestones that shaped SGN Controls into the trusted partner we are today.

2018

Foundation & Vision

Started in Chandigarh by experts in air quality and lab research. Mr. Aditya Garg and his team of industrial experts and researchers laid the foundation with a clear vision to revolutionize scientific instrumentation.

2019

Custom Solutions Begin

Developed custom aerosol monitors and lab automation for Indian research labs. Our first breakthrough products established our reputation for quality and innovation in the scientific community.

2020

Mobile Revolution

First mobile aerosol research labs deployed for field research and industry. This innovation brought laboratory-grade testing capabilities directly to research sites, revolutionizing field studies.

2021

Global Expansion

Entered advanced automation, filter/mask testing, and global markets. Our solutions gained international recognition, leading to partnerships with research institutions worldwide.

2022-2024

Scaling Excellence

Serving 100+ labs, expanding offerings, ongoing innovation in R&D support. Today, we continue to push boundaries with IoT integration, AI-powered analytics, and sustainable research solutions.
